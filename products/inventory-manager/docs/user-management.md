# User Management

Feature ID: inventory.auth.user_management

## Overview
User Management allows authorized admins to view members, change roles, and remove users from a company.

## Tier Availability
- Business
- Enterprise

## How It Works
- The User Management modal lists company members and their roles.
- Admins can change roles for non-super users.
- Admins can remove members to revoke access.

## Limitations
- Super users cannot be removed by other users.
- Role changes apply immediately.
- No bulk role updates or bulk removals.

## Support Expectations
Support may be asked to clarify who can manage users and why certain actions are disabled. Confirm tier eligibility and permissions before advising.
